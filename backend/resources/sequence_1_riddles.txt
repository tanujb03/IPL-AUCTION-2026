Riddle Card 1: The Intense Allrounder

Question: I’ve got a cabinet full of IPL trophies, but I’m probably best known for my intense on-field death stares and my very famous younger brother. I recently traded my Nawabi vibes in Lucknow to join the chaos at the Chinnaswamy. Who am I?

Answer: Krunal Pandya

Riddle Card 2: The Scoop King

Question: I am the absolute "Boss" of scooping terrifyingly fast bowlers right over the keeper's head. I spent years painting the town pink with my centuries, but now I’m taking my English royalty to the biggest cricket stadium in the world. Who am I?

Answer: Jos Buttler
