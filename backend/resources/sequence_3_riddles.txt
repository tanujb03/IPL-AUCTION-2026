Riddle Card 1: The 'Golden Arm' Deity (Grade B)

Question: Mere mortals play cricket, but I am universally recognized by the internet as a 'Lord'. After winning trophies in yellow and touring through Delhi and Kolkata, the 'Palghar Express' is finally pulling into the Wankhede to play for his home city. Who am I?

Answer: Shardul Thakur

Riddle Card 2: The Injury-Defying Giant (Grade C)

Question: I might spend a little too much time hanging out in the physio room, but when I’m fit, my tall left-arm pace is an absolute nightmare. My absolute peak IPL moment was coming back from a massive shoulder injury to defend just 11 runs in the final over against Tim David and Cameron Green. While others shuffle around in auctions, I’ve stayed put to lead the pace attack for the Nawabs in the North. Who am I?

Answer: Mohsin Khan
