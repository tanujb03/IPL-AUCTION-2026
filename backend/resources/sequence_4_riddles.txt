Riddle Card 1: The Super Over Specialist (Grade A)

Question: I burst onto the IPL scene by delivering a legendary, pinpoint Super Over yorker that even peak Andre Russell couldn’t touch. Known for my giant smile and terrifying pace, I formed half of the most feared South African bowling duo in Delhi before packing my bags for Punjab. Now, I’m bringing my fiery heat to the world's biggest stadium to hunt wickets for the Titans. Who am I?

Answer: Kagiso Rabada
